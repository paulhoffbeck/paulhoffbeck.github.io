Engineers Contacted

I had the opportunity to talk with several engineers from my professional and personal network. Here are the insights that struck me the most from our discussions.

Fabien HILBERG

R&D Engineer at KNDS Germany

Key Insight:

“Being an engineer means working with cutting-edge technologies in fields that truly matter. It’s a profession that opens many doors worldwide thanks to the adaptability and versatility it develops.”

Denis GOUDAY

Project Engineer at MBDA

Key Insight:

“What I enjoy most about engineering is project and team management. We work on diverse missions, often abroad. For instance, I’ve had the chance to take part in projects in Egypt and several other countries.”

Laurent COURTE

Solution Engineer at Orange Business

Key Insight:

“This career allows you to choose your path — between high-demand positions or those offering better work-life balance. Being an engineer also means knowing how to adapt your professional journey to each stage of your life.”

Sophian RUBEAU

Head of IP Networks & Cybersecurity Sales at Orange

Key Insight:

“Engineering degrees open doors to positions of responsibility and even teaching opportunities. Curiosity and the willingness to stand out are key: many professional opportunities came from what I did outside of academics.”
