CSR Commitment

Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) plays an increasingly important role in the engineering profession, guiding professional practices toward more sustainable and ethical decision-making. By integrating these concerns early in project design, engineers help reduce environmental impacts, improve stakeholder well-being, and ensure ethical practices aligned with today’s societal expectations. This approach not only enhances the quality of proposed solutions but also helps meet regulatory requirements and address the challenges of ecological transition.

My Volunteer Commitments

Throughout my journey, I’ve had the opportunity to be involved in several volunteer activities, which have increased my awareness of social and environmental issues and taught me the importance of contributing, at my level, to responsible and meaningful initiatives.

Lourdes Hospitality

During my final year of high school, I had the opportunity to serve for a week as a volunteer caregiver at the Lourdes Sanctuary. This experience gave me direct exposure to human challenges related to vulnerability and dependence. By assisting sick or disabled individuals, I developed stronger listening, patience, and service skills. It also made me more aware of the importance of voluntary work based on respect, dignity, and compassion for others.

Les Restos du Cœur

I also volunteered with Les Restos du Cœur, where I became aware of the real challenges of food insecurity and waste. I took part in collecting, sorting, and distributing food supplies, ensuring efficient resource management to minimize losses. This experience taught me the value of a well-organized, solidarity-driven network capable of fighting both scarcity and waste while maximizing the value of every distributed product. It deepened my understanding of the social challenges surrounding food and the importance of collective action in addressing them effectively.

Looking Ahead

As a future engineer, I aim to embed social and environmental responsibility into my professional practice. Aware of the environmental, social, and ethical implications of technical choices, I strive to contribute to the development of sustainable solutions. My goal is to apply eco-design principles, promote responsible innovation, and ensure that the projects I contribute to meet stakeholder expectations while minimizing their environmental footprint.
