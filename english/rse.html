CSR Commitment

CSR commitment is becoming increasingly important in the engineering profession, guiding professional practices toward more sustainable and responsible decisions. By integrating these challenges from the very beginning of project design, engineers help reduce environmental impact, improve stakeholder well-being, and ensure ethical standards aligned with today's societal expectations. This approach not only strengthens the quality of proposed solutions but also helps meet regulatory requirements and address the challenges of ecological transition.

My Community Engagements

Throughout my journey, I have had the opportunity to take part in various community engagements, which have made me aware of social and environmental issues and taught me the importance of contributing—at my own level—to responsible actions.

The Lourdes Hospitality

During my final year of high school, I had the opportunity to serve for a week as a volunteer caregiver at the Lourdes sanctuary. This experience gave me direct insight into the human challenges associated with vulnerability and dependency. By supporting sick or disabled individuals, I developed a heightened sense of listening, patience, and service. It also made me aware of the importance of volunteer work grounded in respect, dignity, and attention to each person.

Restos du Cœur

I also volunteered at the Restos du Cœur, where I became concretely aware of issues related to food insecurity and waste. This experience involved collecting, sorting and distributing food, ensuring rigorous resource management to optimise use and avoid unnecessary losses. It taught me the importance of a solidarity-based organisation capable of fighting both scarcity and waste while valuing every product distributed. This engagement strengthened my understanding of social issues linked to food and the importance of collective initiatives to address them effectively.

What Comes Next?

As a future engineer, I aim to ground my professional actions in a societal responsibility approach. Aware of the environmental, social, and ethical impacts tied to technical choices, I aspire to contribute to the development of sustainable solutions. My goal is to incorporate eco-design principles, promote responsible innovation, and ensure that the projects I take part in meet stakeholder expectations while minimizing their environmental footprint.
